Codemotion Madrid 2018

This was my first attendance at a computer-related event. I liked the experience and I met a lot of people, I got in touch about what the job market offers and wants and I also received a lot of merchandising from companies while listening to their proposals. I even got to see John Romero playing DOOM! The atmosphere was very pleasant and I was able to enjoy the event without problems. I am sure that I will repeat in the future if I can.

Online quiz "Do you know Uzbekistan?"

During my last position as a research support technician, a PhD student from Uzbekistan who was working in the group entered me and other colleagues in a online quiz competition conducted by the COMMITTEE ON INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS AND FRIENDSHIP RELATIONS WITH FOREIGN COUNTRIES UNDER THE CABINET OF MINISTERS OF THE REPUBLIC OF UZBEKISTAN. I came second and, after a waiting time due to COVID-19 restrictions, we received the gifts and sent a photo as proof which was published in the news announcing the prize-giving.

You can read the news of the award ceremony, translated into English by Google Translate, on the COMMITTEE ON INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS AND FRIENDSHIP RELATIONS WITH FOREIGN COUNTRIES UNDER THE CABINET OF MINISTERS OF THE REPUBLIC OF UZBEKISTAN website.
